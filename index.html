Steve Wozniak

Early Life

Born on August 11, 1950 in San Jose, California.
Went to the University of Colorado (1968-69) then dropped out.
Later attended a local community college, then the University of California, Berkeley, in 1971.
He met Steve Jobs while a freshman at Berkeley. Jobs was still in high school. They both became members of the Homebrew Computer Club - this is where the Apple I was conceived of - motherboard-only personal computer.
While at the University of California, he designed the “Blue Box” - a device used for hacking into a phone network without paying for long-distance calls. He and Steve Jobs began selling them to other students.
After landing a position in the Hewlett-Packard company (HP), he dropped out of University in 1975.
